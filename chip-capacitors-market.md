<p><ul><li>Pressure-sensitive</li><li>Thermosensitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chip capacitors market is segmented into pressure-sensitive and thermosensitive types. Pressure-sensitive chip capacitors respond to mechanical stress, altering their capacitance with applied pressure, making them ideal for dynamic sensing applications. In contrast, thermosensitive chip capacitors change their capacitance in response to temperature fluctuations, suitable for thermal management and sensing devices. These capacitors are utilized in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and telecommunications, catering to specific applications that require precise measurements of pressure or temperature variations.</p></p>

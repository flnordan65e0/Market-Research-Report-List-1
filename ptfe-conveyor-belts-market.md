<p><ul><li>PTFE Seamless Conveyor Belts</li><li>PTFE Mesh Conveyor Belts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE conveyor belts market consists of two main types: PTFE seamless conveyor belts and PTFE mesh conveyor belts. PTFE seamless conveyor belts offer a continuous, smooth surface ideal for applications requiring high-temperature resistance and chemical stability. In contrast, PTFE mesh conveyor belts feature openings that allow for airflow and drainage, making them suitable for processes involving moisture or needing cooling. Both types are widely used in industries such as food processing, textiles, and pharmaceuticals due to their durability and versatility.</p></p>

<p><p>Infection control encompasses strategies to eliminate or reduce pathogens in healthcare settings, essential for preventing disease spread. The market is divided into two main segments: disinfection and sterilization. Disinfection involves processes that reduce harmful microorganisms on surfaces and equipment, typically using chemical agents or heat. Sterilization, on the other hand, aims to destroy all forms of microbial life, often employing methods like steam, ethylene oxide, or radiation. Both segments are critical in ensuring safety in clinical environments.</p></p>

<p><p>The bifocal reading glasses market primarily features two types: flap-top bifocals and round bifocals. Flap-top bifocals are characterized by a visible upper section for distance vision, with a semicircular lower segment for reading. This design allows a smooth transition between distances. In contrast, round bifocals have a circular segment at the bottom of the lens dedicated to near vision, providing a more traditional look. Both types cater to various visual needs, enhancing clarity and convenience for users with presbyopia.</p></p>

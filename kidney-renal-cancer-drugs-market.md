<p><p>The kidney and renal cancer drugs market is categorized into three main types: targeted therapy, chemotherapy, and immunotherapy. Targeted therapy involves drugs that specifically attack cancer cells with minimal effect on normal cells, often focusing on molecular abnormalities. Chemotherapy uses cytotoxic substances to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells, though it can impact healthy cells as well. Immunotherapy enhances the body's immune response against cancer, empowering the immune system to recognize and eliminate tumor cells. Each approach offers unique strategies in cancer management.</p></p>

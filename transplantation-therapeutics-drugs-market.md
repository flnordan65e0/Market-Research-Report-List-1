<p><ul><li>Calcineurin Inhibitors</li><li>Anti-Proliferative Agents</li><li>Corticosteroids</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transplantation therapeutics drugs are essential in preventing organ rejection post-transplant. Calcineurin inhibitors, such as cyclosporine and tacrolimus, suppress the immune response. Anti-proliferative agents, including azathioprine and mycophenolate mofetil, inhibit T-cell proliferation to enhance graft acceptance. Corticosteroids, such as prednisone, reduce inflammation and immune activity. Other drugs may include mTOR inhibitors and monoclonal antibodies, which offer additional immunosuppressive mechanisms. Together, these drug categories are vital for improving patient outcomes and ensuring the success of transplant procedures.</p></p>

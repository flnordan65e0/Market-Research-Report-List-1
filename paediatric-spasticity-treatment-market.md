<p><p>The pediatric spasticity treatment market encompasses two primary types: drug and surgical treatments. Surgical treatment involves interventions such as selective dorsal rhizotomy and orthopedic surgeries that aim to reduce spasticity and enhance function. Both approaches target improving quality of life for children with spasticity by alleviating symptoms and promoting better physical capabilities.</p></p>

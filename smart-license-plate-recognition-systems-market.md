<p><p>Smart License Plate Recognition (LPR) systems are categorized into three market types: mobile, fixed, and portable. Mobile systems are typically mounted on vehicles, enabling real-time license plate recognition while in motion, suitable for law enforcement or parking enforcement. Fixed systems are stationary and installed at strategic locations like toll booths or parking lots, providing constant monitoring. Portable systems are handheld devices, allowing flexible use in various locations for tasks such as vehicle verification and monitoring in events or inspections.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Automotive Air Intake Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Air Type</li><li>Ram Air Type</li><li>Short Ram Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air intake module market encompasses three key types: Cold Air Type, Ram Air Type, and Short Ram Type. The Cold Air Type enhances engine performance by drawing cooler air from outside the engine compartment, resulting in increased horsepower. The Ram Air Type utilizes airflow generated by the vehicle’s motion, maximizing intake pressure at high speeds. Meanwhile, the Short Ram Type offers a compact design that improves throttle response by reducing intake path length, though it may draw warmer air. Each type caters to different performance needs and vehicle designs.</p></p>

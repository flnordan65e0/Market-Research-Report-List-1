<p><p>The aerospace industry flaw detectors encompass various types, each serving specific inspection needs. Ultrasonic detectors use high-frequency sound waves to identify internal defects within materials. Eddy current methods utilize electromagnetic induction to detect surface flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic flaw detection involves applying magnetic fields to reveal surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Laser-based techniques provide precise measurements and surface analysis. Other methods may include visual inspections and radiographic testing, ensuring comprehensive safety and reliability in aerospace applications.</p></p>

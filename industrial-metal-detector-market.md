<p><strong>The Industrial Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rectangular Aperture Metal Detectors</li><li>Gravity Fall Metal Detectors</li><li>Pipeline Liquid, Paste and Slurry Metal Detectors</li><li>Metal Detectors With Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial metal detector market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Rectangular aperture metal detectors are ideal for large, flat product detection, ensuring contaminants are identified before packaging. Gravity fall metal detectors are utilized in vertical systems to detect metal in free-falling products. Pipeline liquid, paste, and slurry metal detectors monitor fluid products for contaminants in production lines. Lastly, metal detectors with conveyor systems integrate detection directly into material handling processes, enhancing efficiency and product safety.</p></p>

<p><p>The Radiology Surgical Robots market encompasses various technologies, including X-ray based, gamma-ray based, and proton-beam based systems. X-ray based robots primarily utilize traditional imaging techniques for real-time guidance during surgeries. Gamma-ray based systems leverage radiopharmaceuticals and advanced imaging for targeted therapies, often in oncology. Proton-beam based robots utilize protons for precise tumor targeting, minimizing damage to surrounding tissues. Each type enhances surgical precision and patient safety, addressing specific medical needs and advancing minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>

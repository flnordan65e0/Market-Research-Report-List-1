<p><p>PCI Express (PCIe) market types refer to different generations of the PCIe interface, each offering increased data transfer speeds and improved performance. As technology evolves, newer generations become prevalent in high-performance computing, gaming, and enterprise applications, driving demand for components that can leverage the higher bandwidth and reduced latency offered by each successive generation.</p></p>

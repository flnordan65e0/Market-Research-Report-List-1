<p><ul><li>Potting Solar Cell Junction Boxes</li><li>Non Potting Solar Cell Junction Boxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar cell junction boxes market comprises two main types: potting and non-potting junction boxes. Potting solar cell junction boxes are filled with a resin material to provide enhanced protection against moisture, dust, and environmental factors, ensuring better durability and longevity. In contrast, non-potting solar cell junction boxes do not utilize potting materials, allowing for easier maintenance and repair but may offer less protection. Both types cater to different installation conditions and performance requirements in solar energy systems.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Ultraviolet-Curing Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiation Curing</li><li>Cation Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet-Curing Resin market includes two primary types: Radiation Curing and Cation Curing. Radiation Curing utilizes UV light to initiate a photochemical reaction, allowing for quick drying and hardening of coatings, inks, and adhesives. This method offers fast processing and strong adhesion. Cation Curing, on the other hand, involves the use of cationic photoinitiators, resulting in a chemical reaction that provides excellent resistance to heat and chemicals. Both types are essential for diverse applications in industries like automotive, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>

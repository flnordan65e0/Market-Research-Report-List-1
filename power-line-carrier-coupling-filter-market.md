<p><ul><li>Single-phase Coupling</li><li>Phase-to-phase Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Line Carrier Coupling Filter Market encompasses two primary types: single-phase coupling and phase-to-phase coupling. Single-phase coupling involves connecting the communication signals to one phase of the power line, typically used in low-voltage applications. In contrast, phase-to-phase coupling enables communication between two phases, enhancing signal transmission across higher voltage systems. Both types serve essential roles in enabling reliable data communication over power lines, facilitating applications such as smart grid technologies and remote monitoring systems.</p></p>

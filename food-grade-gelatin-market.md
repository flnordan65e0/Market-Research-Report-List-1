Bone gelatin is sourced from animal bones, offering a rich source of collagen, ideal for jellies and soups. Halal gelatin, made from animals processed according to Islamic law, caters to the growing demand for compliant food products. Each type serves distinct culinary purposes, appealing to various consumer preferences.</p></p>

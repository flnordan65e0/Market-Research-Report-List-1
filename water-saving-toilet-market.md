In contrast, pneumatic toilets operate using air pressure to initiate flushing, offering swift and powerful performance while minimizing water consumption. Both types cater to eco-conscious consumers seeking to reduce water usage without sacrificing performance, contributing to sustainable bathroom solutions in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>

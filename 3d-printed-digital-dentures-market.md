Full dentures replace an entire arch of missing teeth, offering a complete solution for edentulous patients. In contrast, partial dentures are designed for individuals with some remaining natural teeth, providing support and stability while complementing existing dental structures. Both types utilize advanced 3D printing technology for precise customization, improved comfort, and faster production, enhancing patient satisfaction and streamlining dental practices.</p></p>

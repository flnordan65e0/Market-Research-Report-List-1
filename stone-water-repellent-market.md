<p><p>The Stone Water Repellent market is primarily categorized into solvent-based and water-based products. Solvent-based repellents offer strong adhesion and deep penetration into stone surfaces, making them ideal for high-stress environments but may emit volatile compounds. Water-based repellents, on the other hand, are eco-friendly and easier to apply, providing adequate protection while minimizing environmental impact. Both types serve to enhance the durability of stone surfaces by repelling moisture, preventing staining, and reducing degradation caused by water exposure.</p></p>

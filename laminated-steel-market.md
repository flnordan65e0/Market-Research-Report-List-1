<p><strong>The Laminated Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fusion Method Laminated Steel</li><li>Bonding Agent Laminated Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated steel market comprises two primary types: fusion method laminated steel and bonding agent laminated steel. Fusion method laminated steel involves the heat treatment and melting of layers to create a solid bond, resulting in enhanced durability and performance. In contrast, bonding agent laminated steel uses adhesives or resins to bond layers together, offering flexibility in design and reduced weight. Both methods cater to different applications, balancing strength, weight, and cost based on user requirements.</p></p>

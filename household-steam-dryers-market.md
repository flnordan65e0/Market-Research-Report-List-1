<p><p>The household steam dryers market comprises two main types: tumble steam dryers and steam closets. Tumble steam dryers utilize a rotating drum to circulate steam, effectively reducing wrinkles and sanitizing clothing while maintaining fabric quality. In contrast, steam closets are compact units designed for hanging garments, applying steam directly to remove wrinkles and odors. Both types offer convenient solutions for garment care, enhancing household efficiency and providing an alternative to traditional ironing or washing methods.</p></p>

Low pressure hydraulic cylinders typically operate under 150 psi, suitable for basic applications. Medium pressure cylinders operate between 150 psi and 3000 psi, offering a balance of power and efficiency for various industrial uses. High pressure cylinders exceed 3000 psi, providing robust performance for demanding applications in sectors like construction and manufacturing, where precise control and strength are essential.</p></p>

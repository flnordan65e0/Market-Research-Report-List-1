<p><p>High-Integrity Pressure Protection Systems (HIPPS) are critical safety systems designed to prevent overpressure situations in industrial processes. The market encompasses several components: Field Initiators detect pressure levels, Logic Solvers process the data and make decisions, while Valves serve to control fluid flow. Actuators, integral to the system, execute the commands from the logic solver by physically operating the valves. Together, these components ensure operational safety, maintain system integrity, and minimize risks in high-pressure environments across various industries.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li><li>Non-Solvent Epoxy Floor Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy floor coating market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based and non-solvent-based coatings. Solvent epoxy floor coatings contain solvents that enhance application ease and drying speed but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), raising health and environmental concerns. In contrast, non-solvent epoxy floor coatings are low-VOC options that offer better indoor air quality and are often preferred for residential and commercial spaces. Both types provide excellent durability, chemical resistance, and aesthetic appeal for flooring solutions.</p></p>

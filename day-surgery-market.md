<p><p>Day surgery, also known as same-day surgery or outpatient surgery, refers to surgical procedures that allow patients to be admitted, undergo surgery, and be discharged on the same day, without the need for an overnight hospital stay. This approach emphasizes efficiency, reducing hospital stays and associated healthcare costs, while maintaining patient safety and comfort.</p><p>The Day Surgery Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.00% during the forecast period, driven by several factors. Increasing awareness of the benefits of outpatient surgeries, such as faster recovery times and reduced healthcare expenses, is prompting more healthcare providers to adopt this model. Technological advancements in minimally invasive surgical techniques are also playing a crucial role, allowing for safer and more effective procedures that require less recovery time. Moreover, a growing geriatric population coupled with a rise in chronic diseases is leading to a higher demand for surgical interventions. The trend towards value-based healthcare is further propelling the adoption of day surgery, as it aligns with the goals of improving patient outcomes while minimizing costs. Collectively, these factors create a robust landscape for growth in the day surgery market.</p></p>

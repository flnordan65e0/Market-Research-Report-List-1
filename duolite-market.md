<p><p>Duolite market types refer to two main categories of ion exchange resins: cationic and anionic resins. Cationic resins are designed to attract and exchange positively charged ions, effectively removing hardness and impurities from water. Anionic resins, on the other hand, target negatively charged ions, helping to eliminate contaminants like nitrates and sulfates. Both types are essential in water treatment, industrial processes, and various applications where ion removal enhances product quality and compliance with safety standards.</p></p>

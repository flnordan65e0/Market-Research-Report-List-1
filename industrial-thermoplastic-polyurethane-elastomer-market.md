<p><p>The Industrial Thermoplastic Polyurethane Elastomer (TPU) market is categorized into three main types: polyester-based, polyether-based, and others. Polyester-based TPUs offer excellent mechanical properties and abrasion resistance, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Polyether-based TPUs, known for their outstanding resistance to hydrolysis and microorganisms, are ideal for applications in the medical and footwear industries. The "others" category includes specialty TPUs that cater to niche markets, focusing on specific performance characteristics or unique applications.</p></p>

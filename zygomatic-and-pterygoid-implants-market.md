<p><p>Zygomatic and pterygoid implants are specialized dental implants designed to support prosthetic teeth in patients with limited bone in the upper jaw. Zygomatic implants are anchored in the zygomatic bone (cheekbone), while pterygoid implants are placed in the pterygoid region to provide additional stability for restorations. These implants are particularly beneficial for patients who have suffered bone loss due to conditions like periodontal disease or trauma, making traditional implant placement challenging.</p><p>The Zygomatic and Pterygoid Implants Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.

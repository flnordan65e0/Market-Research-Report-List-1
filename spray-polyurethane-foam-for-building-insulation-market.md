<p><ul><li>Open Cell Spray Polyurethane Foam</li><li>Closed Cell Spray Polyurethane Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray polyurethane foam (SPF) insulation is categorized into two main types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell spray polyurethane foam is lightweight, flexible, and offers excellent soundproofing properties, making it ideal for interior applications. It has a lower R-value but is more cost-effective for residential spaces. Closed cell spray polyurethane foam is denser and offers higher insulation values, along with moisture resistance, making it suitable for exterior applications and areas prone to moisture exposure.</p></p>

<p><p>The Borax market comprises three primary types: Anhydrous Borax, Borax Pentahydrate, and Borax Decahydrate. Anhydrous Borax, free of water, is used in high-temperature applications like glass and ceramics. Borax Pentahydrate, containing five water molecules, is favored in detergents and cleaning agents due to its solubility. Borax Decahydrate, with ten water molecules, is widely used in agriculture, cosmetics, and as a flux in metallurgy. Each type serves distinct industries, driven by their unique chemical properties and water content.</p></p>

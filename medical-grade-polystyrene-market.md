<p><p>The medical grade polystyrene market encompasses three main types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS), High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS), and Expanded Polystyrene (EPS). GPPS is valued for its clarity and rigidity, making it suitable for packaging and laboratory equipment. HIPS offers enhanced impact resistance, ideal for durable medical devices. EPS is lightweight and provides excellent insulation, used in protective packaging for sensitive medical products. Each type serves distinct applications within the medical sector, addressing various packaging and product requirements.</p></p>

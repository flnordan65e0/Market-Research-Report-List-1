<p><p>Enterprise Project Management Software (EPM) can be categorized into Cloud-Based and On-Premise solutions. Cloud-Based EPM software offers scalability, accessibility, and automatic updates over the internet, allowing teams to collaborate in real-time from various locations. In contrast, On-Premise EPM software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data and customization, but requiring more IT resources for maintenance. Each market type caters to different business needs regarding security, budget, and project management flexibility.</p></p>

<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li><li>REPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile electric power steering (EPS) systems are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS)** utilizes an electric motor on the steering column, providing direct assistance to the driver’s inputs. </p><p>2. **Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS)** places the motor at the pinion gear, enhancing torque and efficiency, often preferred in compact designs.</p><p>3. **Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS)** integrates the motor directly onto the rack, offering improved responsiveness and feedback for a more connected driving experience.</p></p>

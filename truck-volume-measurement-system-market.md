<p><p>The truck volume measurement system market is divided into two main types: static and dynamic measurement systems. Static measurement systems assess vehicle dimensions and cargo volume when stationary, often using physical measurement tools or scanning technology. In contrast, dynamic measurement systems evaluate truck volume while in motion, employing advanced sensors and imaging technologies to capture data on-the-fly. Both systems serve crucial roles in logistics, allowing for efficient cargo management and optimizing shipping processes based on precise volume measurements.</p></p>

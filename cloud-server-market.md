<p><p>The cloud server market comprises three main types: IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS. IaaS offers virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing businesses to manage infrastructure without physical hardware. PaaS provides a platform for developers to build, test, and deploy applications efficiently, streamlining development processes. SaaS delivers software applications hosted in the cloud, enabling users to access them anytime, anywhere, typically through a subscription model. Together, these models cater to diverse business needs, promoting flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Two Wheeler Fuel Injection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Fuel Injection System</li><li>Port Fuel Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler fuel injection market includes **Direct Fuel Injection (DFI)** and **Port Fuel Injection (PFI)** systems. DFI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency, power, and emissions control, making it ideal for high-performance bikes. PFI injects fuel into the intake port, offering simpler design, lower cost, and reliable performance, suitable for standard commuter bikes. Both systems replace carburetors, improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions, with DFI being more advanced and PFI more cost-effective for mass-market applications.</p></p>

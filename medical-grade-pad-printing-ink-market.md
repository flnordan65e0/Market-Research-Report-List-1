<p><p>The medical grade pad printing ink market comprises two primary types: solvent-based and UV curing inks. Solvent-based inks utilize organic solvents for their viscosity and drying properties, making them suitable for various medical applications with high adhesion to substrates. In contrast, UV curing inks rely on ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process, allowing for quick drying and enhanced durability. Both types are used to print on medical devices and packaging, ensuring high quality and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>

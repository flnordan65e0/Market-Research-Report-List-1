<p><p>Refined Carrageenan (RC) is a natural thickening and gelling agent extracted from seaweed, used in various food and industrial applications. The market includes three main types: Kappa Carrageenan, known for its strong gelling properties and commonly used in dairy products and desserts; Iota Carrageenan, which provides a softer gel and is suitable for products like puddings; and Lambda Carrageenan, offering thickening without gel formation, ideal for dairy and sauces. These types cater to diverse consumer needs across several sectors.</p></p>

<p><p>Fluoro Silicone Rubber (FSR) is a specialized polymer known for its exceptional thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications. The market can be divided into two types: High Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) Type and Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV) Type. HTV FSR, cured under heat, is ideal for high-temperature environments, while RTV FSR cures at room temperature, providing easier processing and applicability in various industries. Both types cater to unique requirements across automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>

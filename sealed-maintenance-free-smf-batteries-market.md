<p><strong>The Sealed Maintenance Free (SMF) Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbed Glass Mat Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealed Maintenance Free (SMF) batteries include Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries and Gel batteries, both designed for reliable, long-term performance without the need for regular maintenance. AGM batteries utilize fiberglass mats to absorb electrolyte, offering enhanced durability and vibration resistance, making them ideal for high-drain applications. Gel batteries, on the other hand, contain a silica-based gel electrolyte, providing superior deep cycling capabilities and improved safety against leakage. Both types are widely used in industries like automotive, renewable energy, and telecommunications for their efficiency and low self-discharge rates.</p></p>

<p><p>The Food & Beverage Plastics Market encompasses various types of plastics used for packaging. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is prevalent for bottles due to its strength and recyclability. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is used for containers and jugs, known for its durability. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is used for some food wraps and containers, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is lightweight, often used in bags. PP (Polypropylene) is versatile, used for cups and food containers, and PS (Polystyrene) is common in disposable cutlery and containers. Each type plays a crucial role in food safety and convenience.</p></p>
